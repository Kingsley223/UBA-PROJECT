Self Service

Explore UBA Group's self-service options to manage your banking effortlessly.

Account Opening

On Leo, send ACCOUNT OPENING on Facebook or WhatsApp and follow the prompts.

Dial your Country USSD Code e.g *919*20# (in Nigeria) to open an account. Please note that you would have to enroll first.

Visit aop.ubagroup.com to open an account.

Check Balance

Send CHECK BALANCE to Leo, authenticate with your PIN and get your account balance.

On Mobile Banking, log in, select banking and view your account balance on the dashboard.

For UBA Internet Banking, log in and view your balance on the dashboard.

Dial your Country USSD Code e.g *919*00# (in Nigeria) on your registered phone to get your balance. Please note that you would have to enroll first.

Get Statement

On Leo, send ACCOUNT STATEMENT and follow the prompts. Please note that you would have to enroll first.

For Mobile Banking, click on self-service and choose bank statement option to generate a statement up to a year.

On UBA Internet banking, click on the My Bank Statement option, choose a date range within one year and download.

Get Internet Banking User ID

On the UBA Internet Banking landing page, click on the forgot User ID option and follow the prompts.

Increase transaction Limit

On Mobile Banking, choose the self-service option from the menu, select transaction limit and follow the prompts.

Reset password on Internet Banking

On the UBA Internet Banking landing page, click on the forgot password option and follow the prompts.

Get Account Number

Send ACCOUNT NUMBER to Leo on Facebook. Please note that you would have to enroll first.

On Mobile Banking, click on settings and choose the App appearance option and unmask your account number.

For UBA Internet Banking, log in and view your account number on the dashboard.

Block Card

Send BLOCK CARD to Leo on Facebook and WhatsApp and block your card.

Dial your Country USSD Code e.g *919*911# (in Nigeria), follow the prompt and block your card.

On Mobile Banking, choose the self-service option from the menu and block your card.

Freeze Account

Send FREEZE ACCOUNT to Leo.

Dial your Country USSD Code e.g *919*911# (in Nigeria) to freeze your account
